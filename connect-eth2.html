Connect your validator to eth2

⚠️ This page concerns the Medalla testnet only. If you have made a mainnet deposit, you do not need to connect your validator to eth2 quite yet. Mainnet Genesis date has been set to December 1st. This page will be updated nearer the time.

To connect your validator to the Medalla testnet, from the nimbus-eth2 repository run:

 ./run-medalla-beacon-node.sh

You'll be asked to enter your Web3 provider URL again.

Note: If your beacon node is already running, you'll need to shut it down gracefully (Ctrl+c) and re-run the above command.

This will build Nimbus and its dependencies, and connect your validator the eth2 network. You should see that the beacon node has launched with your validator attached:
